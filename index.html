About Us

A South End Gem Since 2007

For over 15 years, Star Cleaners & Laundry has been the neighborhood go-to for South End and Dilworth residents. Located in the Sedgefield Shopping Center right next to Harris Teeter on South Boulevard, we're easy to get to and even easier to love.

Now run by Conrad and Monica, we carry on the tradition of exceptional garment care with a personal touch. From your everyday work shirts to your finest suit, your wedding gown, or shoes that need new life — every item gets the attention it deserves.
